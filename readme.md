## What does this do?

- Removes creation of new platforms
- Limits a player to their own platform
- Automatically create a new platform for new players
- Create new platforms whenever a platform is destroyed
- let players respawn on their platforms

This mod creates a challenge where players have to decide how they use their platforms,
and encourages players to work on and improve their own platforms.
